Our Story

Sawubona, Hi there, My name is Amanda Sikhakhane and I am the proud owner and founder of UBUSO Handmade Scrubs and Masks.
UBUSO Handmade Scrubs and Masks is an idea that I got after my own skincare struggles and the struggle to find all-organic products.
Our company prides itself in using all organic ingriedients and hand-making all our skincare products to suit your needs.
We do not only pride ourselves in skincare, but we also pride ourselves in not using harmful chemicals in our products and
in being cruelty-free through not testing our products on animals and lastly through trying our best to not damage the environment
by using harmful packaging. At UBUSO Handmade Scrubs and Masks we cater for everyone regardless of race, gender and sexuality.

Siyabonga, thank you for your continued love and support.We hope to see more of our products be incorporated in
daily skincare routines.
