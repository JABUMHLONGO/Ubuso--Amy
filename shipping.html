Refund and Return Policy

At UBUSO we strive for customer satisfaction and to provide quality always.Our Return policy allows you the customer to return
any goods that you are not satisfied with. The product can be returned if it is damaged or you might have unfortunately received an incorrect order.
If this happens the customer is eligible to receive his/her refund or wait for the correct order to be proccessed with a little apology gift on us.
Please note that if you received an incorrect, you need to return it unused to us.Using the product automatically disqualifies you from returning it.
Pease notify us within 5 days of delivery about any damaged and incorrect orders.
